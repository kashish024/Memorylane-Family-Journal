Frequently asked questions

Is MemoryLane free?

Yes. The free tier includes one child, up to 20 memories per child per month, and manual monthly summaries. Premium unlocks unlimited memories, contributors, automatic summaries, and backups.

Who can see my family's memories?

Only you and people you explicitly invite as contributors. Your data is stored securely and tied to your account. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do monthly summaries work?

MemoryLane uses AI to read through the month's memories and craft a warm narrative summary. Free users can generate them manually; Premium subscribers receive them automatically by email.

Can grandparents add memories?

Yes, with MemoryLane Premium. Send an invitation from the app and they can contribute photos and notes to your child's timeline.
